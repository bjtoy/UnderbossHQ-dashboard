THE GRAND MAFIA: RAID PREPARATION & EXECUTION
:::

:::color-gold
Faction Edition — Advanced Strategy & Execution
:::

Raids are coordinated faction attacks against high-value PvE or PvP targets, including enemy turfs, Kingpins, Mercenaries, and event bosses. They allow multiple members to contribute troops to a single rally, led by one player.

:::warning
The rally leader's gear, enforcers, combat specs, and boosts determine the raid's overall strength.
:::

:::section
WHAT RAIDS ARE & WHY THEY MATTER
:::

Raids are essential for:

• Faction dominance in turf wars and events
• Resource denial and enemy suppression
• High-tier loot from PvE raid events
• Faction coordination and growth

:::section
RAID PREPARATION — RALLY LEADERS
:::

• **Mansion Level:** Higher levels = larger rally capacity
• **Crew Capacity:** Maximize via investments and Godfather gear
• **Gear:** Equip a full 6-piece combat set (Victor, New Blood, Kings, Godfather)
• **Godfather Equipment:** Equip a piece that boosts your main troop type or rally size
• **Combat Specs:** Activate your Combat Spec preset (not Economy or Kingpin)
• **Boosts:** Use 25% or 50% ATK, DEF, HP, and March Size boosts
• **Enforcers:** Select 4 enforcers that match your troop type and synergize well
• **Troop Type:** Choose mono-type (e.g. all Bikers) for synergy
• **Scout the Target:** Check for reinforcements, troop types, and traps

:::section
RAID PREPARATION — RALLY JOINERS
:::

• **Troop Type:** Send only the troop type the leader is boosting
• **No Enforcers:** Only the rally leader's enforcers are used
• **Combat Specs:** Activate your Combat Spec preset for troop stat boosts
• **Boosts:** Optional for PvE; recommended for PvP
• **Troop Tier:** Use T9 or T10 for maximum impact
• **Join Early:** Late joins may be excluded from the rally

:::warning
Only the rally leader's enforcers are used. Send only the troop type requested by the rally leader.
:::

**KINGS SET** — Combat | Rally Capacity, All Troop Stats | Unlock UB 60+ | Best for high-end raids

**GODFATHER SET** — Combat | Balanced ATK/DEF/HP | Unlock UB 60+ | Best for general PvP

**CHALLENGER SET** — PvE | Kingpin Damage, Enforcer ATK | Early game | Best for Kingpin fights

:::section
SET BONUSES
:::

• **2 pieces:** Minor stat boost (e.g. +5% ATK)
• **4 pieces:** Moderate boost (e.g. +10% DEF)
• **6 pieces:** Major boost (e.g. +15% HP, +March Size)

:::tip
Only matching set pieces count toward bonuses. Mixing sets disables set bonuses.
:::

:::section
COMBAT SPECS FOR RAIDING
:::

**Rally leaders — prioritize:**
• Troop ATK/DEF/HP for your main troop type
• Rally Capacity
• Troop Damage Bonus
• Enemy Damage Reduction

**Rally joiners — focus on:**
• Troop ATK/DEF/HP for the troop type you're sending
• March Speed (optional)
• Ignore Rally Capacity (only affects leaders)

:::important
Always double-check your active spec before launching or joining a raid. Use presets to switch between Combat, Kingpin, and Economy specs.
:::

:::section
BOOSTS TO ACTIVATE
:::

• **Attack Boost (25% / 50%):** Increases troop damage — leaders mandatory; joiners for PvP
• **Defense Boost:** Reduces incoming damage — optional, PvP only
• **Crew HP Boost:** Increases survivability — useful in PvP
• **March Capacity Boost:** Increases troops you can send — rally leaders only
• **Rally Capacity Boost:** Increases rally size — leaders only (gear/specs)

:::section
TROOP COMPOSITION & TACTICAL VARIANTS
:::

**MONO-TYPE RAID** — All one troop type (e.g. Bikers). Use for most raids (synergy + max damage).

**MIXED-TYPE RAID** — Two troop types (e.g. Biker + Bruiser). Use when countering specific enemy setups.

**TANK-HEAVY RAID** — Bruiser-heavy frontlines. Use for breaking traps or defending.

**SPEED RAID** — Biker-focused, fast march. Use for tile hits, baiting, fast PvP.

:::section
RAID EXECUTION
:::

**Scouting**
Scout before launching to check for reinforcements, anti-scout, and troop types. Cancel or adjust if it's a trap.

**Communication**
• Assign roles: rally leader, reinforcers, backup rallies
• Share rally links early
• Confirm troop type and joiner readiness

**Timing**
• Coordinate rally time in faction chat or Discord
• Use teleports to position near the target
• Consider fake rallies to bait or confuse enemies

:::section

:::section
POST-RAID STRATEGY
:::

• Review battle reports: troop performance, damage dealt, and losses
• Heal wounded troops immediately
• Share reports with the faction for learning
• Adjust enforcers, specs, or gear based on results
• Coordinate follow-up raids or reinforcements

:::color-gold

:::

Prepare correctly. Coordinate clearly. Analyze results. Improve continuously.
