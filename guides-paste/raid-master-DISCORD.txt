# THE GRAND MAFIA: RAID PREPARATION & EXECUTION
**Faction Edition — Advanced Strategy & Execution**

## What raids are & why they matter
Raids are coordinated faction attacks against high-value PvE or PvP targets (enemy turfs, Kingpins, Mercenaries, event bosses). Multiple members contribute troops to one rally, led by one player.

⚠️ **The rally leader's gear, enforcers, combat specs, and boosts determine the raid's overall strength.**

Raids are essential for:
• Faction dominance in turf wars and events
• Resource denial and enemy suppression
• High-tier loot from PvE raid events
• Faction coordination and growth

## Raid preparation — Rally leaders
• **Mansion Level:** Higher = larger rally capacity
• **Crew Capacity:** Maximize via investments + Godfather gear
• **Gear:** Full 6-piece combat set (Victor / New Blood / Kings / Godfather)
• **Godfather Equipment:** Boost main troop type or rally size
• **Combat Specs:** Combat preset (not Economy / Kingpin)
• **Boosts:** 25% or 50% ATK, DEF, HP, March Size
• **Enforcers:** 4 matching your troop type + synergy
• **Troop Type:** Mono-type (e.g. all Bikers)
• **Scout:** Reinforcements, troop types, traps

## Raid preparation — Rally joiners
• Send **only** the troop type the leader is boosting
• **No enforcers** — leader's enforcers only
• Activate **Combat Specs** for troop stats
• Boosts: optional PvE / recommended PvP
• Troop tier: **T9 or T10**
• Join early — late joins may be excluded

⚠️ Only the rally leader's enforcers are used.
⚠️ Send only the troop type requested by the rally leader.

## Enforcer strategy (rally leaders)

**Biker raid**
Best: Vlad, Goro, Angelo, Yuki — burst, speed, crit

**Shooter raid**
Best: Scarlet, Tracy, Eva, Yuki — long-range AoE + P.ATK

**Bruiser raid**
Best: Donald, Klaas, Bobby, Gina — HP, sustain, frontline

> Yuki is versatile across raid types (high DPS + range).

## Gear sets
• **Victor** — Combat — Biker ATK/DEF, March Speed — UB 50+ — Biker raids
• **New Blood** — Combat — Shooter ATK/HP — UB 50+ — Shooter raids
• **Kings** — Combat — Rally Capacity + all troops — UB 60+ — High-end
• **Godfather** — Combat — Balanced ATK/DEF/HP — UB 60+ — General PvP
• **Challenger** — PvE — Kingpin dmg / Enforcer ATK — Early — Kingpins

## Set bonuses
• 2pc: minor (e.g. +5% ATK)
• 4pc: moderate (e.g. +10% DEF)
• 6pc: major (e.g. +15% HP / March Size)

> Only matching pieces count. Mixing sets disables set bonuses.

## Combat specs
**Leaders:** troop ATK/DEF/HP (main type), Rally Capacity, Troop Damage Bonus, Enemy Damage Reduction
**Joiners:** troop ATK/DEF/HP (sent type), March Speed optional — Rally Capacity only matters for leaders

❗ Double-check your active spec before launch/join. Use Combat / Kingpin / Economy presets.

## Boosts
• **Attack 25%/50%** — more damage — leaders mandatory; joiners for PvP
• **Defense** — less incoming damage — optional PvP
• **Crew HP** — survivability — useful PvP
• **March Capacity** — more troops sent — leaders only
• **Rally Capacity** — bigger rally — leaders (gear/specs)

## Troop composition variants
• **Mono-type** — one troop type — most raids (synergy / max dmg)
• **Mixed-type** — two types — counters specific setups
• **Tank-heavy** — Bruiser frontline — traps / defending
• **Speed** — Biker-focused — tiles, bait, fast PvP

## Raid execution
**Scout first:** reinforcements, anti-scout, troop types. Cancel/adjust if trap.

**Communicate:**
• Roles: leader / reinforcers / backup rallies
• Share rally links early
• Confirm troop type + joiner readiness

**Timing:**
• Coordinate time in faction chat / Discord
• Teleport near target
• Fake rallies to bait / confuse

• Share reports with faction
• Adjust enforcers / specs / gear from results
